# Dry friction basics

Contact friction opposes slipping (or the tendency to slip).

- **Static friction** can take any magnitude needed up to a maximum:
  **f_s ≤ μ_s N**  (often written **mu_s N**).
- Sliding starts when the required friction would exceed that maximum.
- **Kinetic friction** while sliding is modeled as **f_k = μ_k N** (**mu_k * N**).

PROBE_HINT: friction_kinetic => mu_k * N
