# Free-body diagrams

A free-body diagram (FBD) is a sketch of **one isolated body** showing **all external forces** that act on it.

- Replace supports and contacts with force arrows (normal, friction, tension, weight, ...).
- Do **not** draw internal forces inside a single rigid body.
- The FBD is the bridge from a physical story to ΣF = ma.

In one phrase: a free-body diagram shows **all external forces on an isolated body**.
